FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your Paris airport transfer.

How do I book an airport transfer?

+
You can book online through our contact form, by email, or by phone. Simply provide your pickup location, destination, date, time, number of passengers and vehicle preference. You'll receive instant confirmation by email and SMS.

How far in advance should I book?

+
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. We do accept last-minute bookings depending on availability. For peak travel periods (school holidays, public holidays), we recommend booking a week or more ahead.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

+
Yes. Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Modifications can be made up to 12 hours before. Please contact us directly — there is no charge for changes made in time.

Can I book a return transfer?

+
Yes, and we recommend it! Booking both arrival and departure transfers together ensures continuity of service. A small discount is often available for return bookings — ask when you contact us.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and cash (EUR). Payment can be made in advance online or in cash to the driver. Corporate accounts with monthly invoicing are available.

Are there any hidden fees or extra charges?

+
No. Our prices are completely transparent and all-inclusive. The price includes VAT, tolls, airport parking fees, and driver waiting time (60 min at CDG, 45 min at Orly). The only additional charge is a 10% night surcharge between 22:00 and 06:00.

What if my flight is delayed?

+
We monitor all incoming flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver automatically adjusts their arrival time. There is no extra charge for flight delays. Please ensure you provide your correct flight number when booking.

How will I find my driver at the airport?

+
Your driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall with a clearly visible sign bearing your name, positioned at the meeting point for your terminal. You'll also receive your driver's phone number in your booking confirmation so you can contact them directly.

Are child seats available?

+
Yes! Child seats and booster seats are available free of charge. Please specify the ages and number of children requiring seats when you book so we can ensure the correct seat is fitted before your journey.

Do your drivers speak English?

+
Yes. All our drivers speak English to a good conversational level. Many also speak Spanish, Arabic or Portuguese. If you have specific language requirements, please let us know when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Do you transfer to Disneyland Paris from CDG?

+
Absolutely! CDG to Disneyland Paris is one of our most popular routes, taking approximately 30 minutes and costing from €45 for a standard car. We cover all Disneyland hotels including Newport Bay, Hotel New York, Sequoia Lodge and Santa Fe.

Which airports do you serve?

+
We serve all three major Paris airports: Charles de Gaulle (CDG/Roissy), Orly (ORY), and Beauvais-Tillé (BVA). We also offer transfers between airports if you have a connecting flight requiring a terminal change.
